Wanna Play The Guessing Game?

Here is what you do!

  1. The computer is going to generate a number between 1 and a 100.
  2. You enter a number, trying to guess the right one.
  3. If you are too low or too high the computer will notify you and let you re-guess.
  4. If you guess right, you win and you get 1 point
